Privacy Policy

Petal — The Free, 100% Offline & Privacy-First Period Tracker

Last Updated: March 13, 2026

🔒 We do not collect, store, transmit, or sell any of your personal or health data. Your data never leaves your device.

Thank you for choosing Petal. This Privacy Policy describes how information is handled in the Petal mobile application on Android and iOS. We have designed Petal with a privacy-by-design philosophy — all data remains strictly on your device.

1. Health & Fitness Data — Disclosure

Petal is a period and menstrual cycle tracking application. Because it is a health app, we are providing a full and explicit disclosure of every category of health and fitness data the app handles:

Data Category Accessed? Collected / Transmitted? Purpose
Menstrual cycle dates Yes — entered by user No. Stored only on device. Core cycle tracking & predictions
Symptoms (e.g. cramps, mood, flow) Yes — entered by user No. Stored only on device. Symptom logging & cycle insights
Notes / journal entries Yes — entered by user No. Stored only on device. Personal health journaling
Predicted ovulation & fertile windows Computed on-device No. Calculated & stored locally only. Cycle prediction
Body/health metrics (if logged) Yes — entered by user (optional) No. Stored only on device. Optional personal health log

No health data is ever transmitted to us, to any third party, or to any server. Petal does not connect to the internet. All computation and storage is performed entirely on your local device.

2. Information We Collect

  • No Personal Data: We do not ask for your name, email, phone number, or social media login.
  • No Health Data Collection: The dates, symptoms, and notes you enter are never transmitted to us or anyone else.
  • No Device Data: We do not collect your IP address, device identifier, or location.
  • No Usage Analytics: We do not track how you use the app.

3. How Your Data Is Stored

All information you enter into Petal is stored locally on your device's internal memory using the device's own secure local storage. Because the app works entirely offline:

  • Your data never leaves your phone.
  • We (the developers) have zero access to your data at any time.
  • If you delete the app, your data is permanently deleted from the device unless you have a system-level backup.

4. App Permissions

Petal requests only the minimum permissions necessary to function:

Permission Reason
Notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS) To send local, on-device cycle reminders. No server is involved.
Internet (INTERNET) Required only for Google Play billing (one-time purchase). No health data is transmitted.

Petal does not request access to: Contacts, Camera, Microphone, Location, Files, or any external health APIs (e.g. Google Fit, Apple Health).

5. Accounts & Cloud Sync

Petal operates without user accounts. There is no sign-up or login. We do not offer cloud synchronisation, meaning your sensitive health information is never stored on external servers.

6. Third-Party Services

  • No Analytics: We do not use Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, or any similar SDK.
  • No Advertising: The app contains no advertising SDKs and displays no ads.
  • No Tracking: We do not track your behaviour across other apps or websites.
  • Payments only: In-app purchases are handled by Google Play Billing and Apple App Store. We never see your payment details.

7. Notifications

Petal may send notifications to remind you of your cycle predictions. These notifications are scheduled entirely on your device. No external server triggers, receives, or processes these messages.

8. Petal Pro & Payments

Advanced features are available as a one-time purchase processed securely by the official Google Play Store and Apple App Store billing systems. Petal developers never see, collect, or store your real name or payment details.

9. Children's Privacy

Petal is a general-audience application. Because we do not collect, store, or process personal data — including from children under the age of 13 — the app is designed to comply with applicable privacy laws including COPPA and GDPR.

10. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade any user data with third parties. There is no data to share — it never reaches us in the first place.

11. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Because Petal collects no personal data and has no servers storing your information, traditional data-subject requests (access, deletion, portability) are handled directly on your own device. To delete all your data, simply uninstall the app. For any questions, contact us below.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Since we do not collect email addresses, we advise you to review this page periodically. Changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.

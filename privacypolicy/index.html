Your health data is yours alone. Petal is built with a privacy-by-design philosophy so your data never leaves your device.

Last Updated: March 8, 2026

Thank you for choosing Petal. We believe that your health data is personal and belongs only to you. We have designed our app with a “privacy-by-design” philosophy, ensuring that your data remains strictly on your device.

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled in the Petal mobile application on Android and iOS. In short: We do not collect, store, or share your data.

1. Information We Collect

  • ▸No Personal Data: We do not ask for your name, email, phone number, or social media login.
  • ▸No Health Data Collection: The dates, symptoms, and notes you enter are not transmitted to us.
  • ▸No Device Data: We do not collect your IP address, device ID, or location.

2. How Your Data Is Stored

All information you enter into Petal is stored locally on your device's internal memory. Because the app works entirely offline:

  • ▸Your data never leaves your phone.
  • ▸We (the developers) have no access to your data.
  • ▸If you delete the app, your data will be permanently deleted from the device unless you have performed your own system-level backup.

3. Accounts & Cloud Sync

Petal operates without user accounts. There is no signup or login process. Consequently, we do not offer cloud synchronization services. This ensures that your sensitive health information is never stored on external servers.

4. Third-Party Services

To maintain your privacy, Petal contains:

  • ▸No Analytics: We do not use Google Analytics, Firebase, or similar services to track how you use the app.
  • ▸No Advertising: The app contains no advertising SDKs (Software Development Kits) and displays no ads.
  • ▸No Tracking: We do not track your behavior across other apps or websites.

5. Notifications

Petal may send notifications to your device to remind you of your cycle predictions. These notifications are scheduled locally on your device. No external server triggers these messages.

6. Petal Pro & Payments

Advanced features are available as a one-time purchase. All transactions are handled securely and anonymously by the official Google Play Store and Apple App Store billing systems. Petal developers never see, collect, or store your real name or payment details.

7. Children's Privacy

Petal is a general audience application. Because we do not collect, store, or process personal data, including from children under the age of 13, the app is designed to be compliant with applicable privacy laws such as COPPA and GDPR.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Since we do not collect email addresses, we advise you to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.
